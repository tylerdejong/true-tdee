# TrueTDEE SEO Checklist

## Technical SEO

- Confirm each calculator page has a unique title and meta description.
- Confirm canonical URLs resolve correctly after deploying.
- Submit `/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console.
- Confirm `/robots.txt` allows crawling.
- Test FAQ schema with Google's Rich Results Test.
- Check mobile usability in Search Console.
- Run Lighthouse for performance, accessibility, best practices, and SEO.
- Add a real production domain through `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL`.
- Verify no placeholder AdSense IDs are deployed.
- Confirm static export pages return 200 status codes.

## On-Page SEO

- Keep the calculator visible near the top of every calculator page.
- Keep H1 text aligned with the search intent of each slug.
- Add internal links between wearable, maintenance, deficit, protein, and timeline pages.
- Expand page-specific examples after launch using Search Console queries.
- Add concise image alt text where images are informative.
- Avoid medical claims and supplement recommendations.
- Keep disclaimers visible but not alarmist.
- Refresh content when formulas, wearable app labels, or platform UI names change.

## Monetisation SEO

- Keep ad density moderate, especially above the fold.
- Do not place sticky mobile ads where they block calculator inputs.
- Review Google AdSense policies before enabling sticky mobile ads.
- Keep ad placeholders out of structured data.
- Confirm page speed remains strong after ads load.

## Content Quality

- Use practical examples with 28, 60, and 90 day tracking periods.
- Explain limitations of short data windows.
- Explain why wearable calorie estimates can differ from observed TDEE.
- Avoid promising exact fat-loss timelines.
- Encourage sustainable targets and professional guidance where appropriate.
